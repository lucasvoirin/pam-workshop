Deploying recorders in the field is an important step in passive acoustic monitoring. It is necessary to take into account certain elements before going into the field.  

#### Installation and programming constraints of recorders  

- [[species|Target species]]: this influences recording locations and periods, as well as acoustic parameters  
- Target environment: aquatic environment, remote areas, etc.  
- Target hours and periods: depends on the [[research_question/index|research question]] and the [[species|target species]]  
- Duration: depends on battery life, recording frequency, etc.  

#### Hardware solutions  

There are several hardware solutions for passive recording. The choice of equipment will depend on the monitoring constraints, as well as on logistical or financial limitations.  

#### [[audio_parameters|Acoustic parameters]]  

Each recorder can be configured so that the characteristics of the recordings meet the constraints.
